Qualification?

  • Be at least 23 years old
  • Minimum of 6 months recent verifiable tractor trailer experience
  • Have a valid class (A) CDL
  • Meet DOT requirements
  • Pass a company physical, including substance screening
  • No DWI/DUI convictions(last 5 years)
  • No more than 3 moving violations in 36 months
  • No major violations in 36 months
